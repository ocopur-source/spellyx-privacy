# Privacy Policy — Spellyx

**Last updated: May 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Spellyx ("we", "our", or "the app") is an educational spelling game designed for children and adults. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Spellyx is designed with privacy first:

- ❌ We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, address)
- ❌ We do **not** collect location data
- ❌ We do **not** require account registration
- ❌ We do **not** share data with third parties
- ❌ We do **not** show personalized advertisements
- ❌ We do **not** track user behavior across apps

## 3. Information Stored Locally

All app data is stored **only on your device** using Android SharedPreferences:

- Word progress and learned words
- Game score and streak count
- Selected theme (light/dark)
- Achievement badges earned

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Spellyx is suitable for children under 13. In compliance with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- No account creation is required
- No personal data is transmitted to our servers
- All content is age-appropriate

## 5. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| `INTERNET` | Required for text-to-speech service |

No other device permissions are requested.

## 6. Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to pronounce words. This processing happens entirely on-device. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

## 7. Third-Party Services

Spellyx does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## 8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security settings (screen lock, encryption).

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this document.
